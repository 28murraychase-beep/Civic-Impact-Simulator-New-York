# CivicImpact

A nonpartisan policy impact simulator — run real fiscal, economic, and political
tradeoff analysis on public policy proposals, entirely in your browser.

**[→ Open the live site](your-github-pages-link-here)**

## What's inside
- **National Policy Library** — 15 nationally-calibrated models (housing, healthcare,
  immigration, carbon tax, paid leave, and more), each with sourced assumptions
- **Build Your Own Scenario** — a generic engine (11 program mechanisms × 5 funding
  sources) that can model almost any real-world policy proposal, not just the presets
- **NY County Housing Explorer** — real population, home values, property tax rates,
  and voter registration data for all 62 New York counties
- **Featured Case Study** — a fully worked example (NY's $335M bond reallocation
  question) showing the model end to end
- **Sources & Methodology** — every document, dataset, and assumption is cited and
  rated by confidence level
